The Lego Effect

Snap out the old one and then snap and twist in a replacement until you hear the click.

Snap, Twist, Bam

Remember Legos? Those wonderful, amazing pieces of plastic that brought out the creative side out of all of us? Well they REALLY brought the creative side out of us here at Team mXers. Our earbuds work similarly to the way Legos do in the sense of modularity. You broke one of the buds on the bus? Snap out the old one and then snap and twist in a replacement until you hear the click. The same goes for the wires, put them into the splitter then just snap and twist; everything works like new. Whether you want to customize the earbuds for a holiday, your #ootd, or they just need fixing... Snap it. Twist. BAM.

Product Features

mXers are split into 6 parts: 1 aux cable, 1 splitter, 2 connecting wires, and 2 buds. This design allows for simple and fluid attatchment and detachment of the product.
