Welcome to mXers

We at mXers are dedicated to giving our customers a completely tailered and individual listening experience.

Not only do we produce headphones, but also inner earbuds. Our products come in customizable sets, complete with a fully disassembled pair of earbuds and one additional peice of each part.

Snap out the old one and then snap and twist in a replacement until you hear the click. The same goes for the wires, put them into the splitter then just snap and twist.

We really care about our people and we can prove it.

Bharat Pulgam is a junior at Wayzata High School who is currently involved in multiple different projects and holds leadership positions at his high school. In the winter of 2015 Bharat started mXers after breaking multiple pairs of headphones and being frustrated at paying a high premium for good sound quality and appearance. After taking his idea to an incubator program called catapult in Chicago his idea was received well at an promising investor pitch where his team was given lots of positive feedback which propelled bharat to restart mXers in Minneapolis. Working since December 2014 Bharat has worked hard to make sure mXers is able to deliver the best possible listening experience to the everyday listers in today’s world.
